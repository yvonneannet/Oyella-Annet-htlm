My first week at Akirachix

Hello, my name is Oyella Annet Eyvone.
My first week at Akirachix was both exciting and daunting.
I was relieved that I had finally made it to the campus, while being scared of what to expect from the coming days. I also got introduced to new things and had some time to quikly adjust to the new environment also to the way activities were run.

Positive Experience

  1. I loved how the allumni and the other staff I got to meet were welcoming, this helped calm my already tense nerves.
  2. The pep talk that was offered by our different allumni in charge of our rooms helped give us direction on how things were run in this campus.
  3. The housekeepers were also so warm and welcoming.
  4. I loved the whole atmosphere of the place, it was calm and one could alwys have the feel of having a warm away from home experience.

Negative Experience

  • My tummy has been hurting since the second day I reported.
  • In the first week I had issues with having to clean after people before I could get to use the toilet.
  • The was no known schedule or timetable in the initial days so it all left me confused on what activity was meant to be done during a specific time.
